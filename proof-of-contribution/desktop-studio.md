# Desktop Contribution Studio

The Desktop Contribution Studio is the bridge between captured material and production-grade AI assets.

## Import

When a BonzAI+ Contribution Pack is imported, Desktop can:

- Validate the pack schema.
- Preview records.
- Detect duplicates.
- Score quality.
- Review licenses and permissions.
- Split records into training and evaluation sets.
- Attach contributor metadata.
- Add the pack to the local contribution ledger.

## Local Ledger

The local ledger tracks relationships between:

- Imported contribution packs.
- Dataset records.
- Training runs.
- Generated outputs.
- Model adapters.
- Minted content.
- Companion/skill actions.
- Revenue attribution.

The ledger is local-first. It can exist before anything is published onchain.

## Training

Training records can become:

- Local-only training data.
- Exported JSONL/dataset artifacts.
- Training NFTs.
- Fine-tune/model token launch inputs.
- Contribution registry records.

## Publishing

Publishing requires:

- A wallet.
- Registry contract addresses configured in the app.
- Metadata/media upload through IPFS/Pinata or configured IPFS-compatible storage.
- User confirmation.

If registry addresses are not configured, the local ledger and training workflow still work.
